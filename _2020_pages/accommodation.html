Accommodation

Hotels

Different types and levels of hotels can be found near the campus.

  • About 10 rooms of the guest house of Kindai University will be provided.
  • The nearest hotel is U-Community hotel at Kintetsu Kawachi-Kosaka Station, next to
  • Yaenosato Station.
  • There are a large number of hotels in Uehonmachi and Namba Area. All the hotel costs
  • about 100 US$.

Welcome Party

The welcome party will be held at the LOUNGE in the academic theater on the main campus. The LOUNGE is within 5 minutes walking distance from the conference venue, Blossom Cafe.

Social event

As the social event, we will take the participants to Namba area. In this area, three big game-centers are located within 100 meters, which are operated by major Japanese arcade game companies: BANDAI NAMCO, SEGA, and Taito. Each game center is a multi-storey building.

Banquet

The banquet will be held at the Swissotel Nankai, where is within 7 minutes walking distance from the game centers.
