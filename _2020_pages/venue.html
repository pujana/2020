Host Institution Profile

Kindai University hosts 34,000 students in bachelor, master and graduate programs, and 2300 scientists. One of the biggest universities in Japan. In 2018, Kindai University is ranked '8' among Japanese private university in THE-Ranking and is ranked 40 of all the Japanese Universities. Department of Informatics is planning to reorganize to Faculty of Informatics in 2020, in order to pursue the advanced AI and IoT research.
