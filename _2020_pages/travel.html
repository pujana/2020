Travel information

The conference will be held in the main campus (Higashi-Osaka Campus) of Kindai University. This campus is located east side of Osaka. A brief instruction for getting to the Higashi-Osaka Campus can be found on: https://www.kindai.ac.jp/english/access/index.html.

How to get to Osaka

A typically recommended way to get to Osaka is to arrive at Kansai International Airport (KIX) by plane, which is one of major international airports in Japan and is located in Osaka. KIX provides direct services to more than 80 cities and 20 countries/regions throughout the world, including many cities in Japan. The airport, which acts as an Asian hub and serves a large number of flights to the Asia-Pacific region, is conveniently located within 30 minutes by train to and from downtown Osaka.

There is also a domestic airport, Osaka International Airport (ITM), in Osaka. ITM does not have any international flights, but you can get there by using domestic flights from other airports in Japan.

Apart from KIX and ITM, there are other two major international airports in Japan: Narita Airport (NRT), and Haneda Airport (HND), which are both located in Tokyo. You can get to Osaka from NRT and HND by train (Shinkansen) or get to KIX or ITM via domestic flights.

From Narita Airport (NRT) to Osaka (JR Shin-Osaka Station)

Train (JR) + Shinkansen
  1. Narita Airport -> Tokyo Station by Narita Express (Approx. 56 min. 3,020 JPY)
  2. Tokyo Station -> Shin-Osaka Station by Shinkansen Super Express (Approx. 150min., 13,620 JPY)
Bus + Shinkansen
  1. Narita Airport -> Tokyo Station by Airport Limousine Bus (Approx. 60 min. 3,100 JPY)
  2. Tokyo Station -> Shin-Osaka Station by Shinkansen Super Express (Approx. 150min., 13,620 JPY)

From Haneda Airport (HND) to Osaka (JR Shin-Osaka Station)

Train (Private railway) + Shinkansen
  1. Haneda Airport -> Shinagawa Station by Keikyu-Kuko Line (Approx. 11 min. 410 JPY)
  2. Shinagawa Station -> Shin-Osaka Station by Shinkansen Super Express (Approx. 146 min. 13,620 JPY)
Bus + Shinkansen
  1. Haneda Airport -> Shin-Yokohama Station by Airport Limousine Bus (Approx. 50 min. 840 JPY)
  2. Shin-Yokohama Station -> Shin-Osaka Station by Shinkansen Super Express (Approx. 134 min. 13,290 JPY)

How to get to Kindai University

You can get to Kindai University by using public transport from Kansai International Airport, Osaka International Airport, and Shin-Osaka Station.

From Kansai International Airport (KIX) to Kindai University

Bus + Train
  1. Kansai International Airport -> Kintetsu Uehonmachi Station by Airport Limousine Bus (Approx. 55 min. 1,550 JPY)
  2. Kintetsu Uehonmachi Station -> Nagase Station by Kintetsu Osaka Line (Approx. 11 min. 260 JPY)
  3. On foot (Approx. 10 min.)
Train
  1. Kansai International Airport -> Tennoji Station by JR Kansai-airport express “Haruka” (Approx. 33 min, 1,710 JPY)
  2. Tennoji Station -> Tsuruhashi Station by JR Osaka Loop Line (Approx. 5 min. 120 JPY)
  3. Tsuruhashi -> Nagase by Kintetsu Nara Line (Approx. 8 min. 210 JPY)
  4. On foot (Approx. 10 min.)

From Osaka International Airport (ITM) to Kindai University

Bus + Train (Recommended)
  1. Osaka Internatioal Airport -> Kintetsu Uehonmachi Station by Airport Limousine Bus (Approx. 35 min. 640 JPY)
  2. Kintetsu Uehonmachi Station -> Nagase Station by Kintetsu Osaka Line (Approx. 11 min. 260JPY)
  3. On foot (Approx. 10 min.)
Train
  1. Osaka International Airport -> Hotarugaike Station by Osaka Monorail
  2. Hotarugaike Station -> Umeda Station by Hankyu Takaraduka Line
  3. Umeda Station -> Osaka Station by walk (Approx. 5 min.)
  4. Osaka Station -> Kyobashi Station by JR Kansai-Airport Rapid Service (Approx. 7 min.)
  5. Kyobashi Station -> Tsuruhashi Station by JR Osaka Loop Line (Approx. 8 min. 220 JPY including Osaka Station -> Kyobashi Station)
  6. Tsuruhashi Station -> Nagase Station by Kintetsu Osaka Line (Approx. 8 min. 210 JPY)
  7. On foot (Approx. 10 min.)

From Shin-Osaka Station to Kindai University

Train
  1. Shin-Osaka Station -> Osaka Station by JR Kyoto Line (Approx. 4 min.)
  2. Osaka Station -> Tsuruhashi Station by JR Osaka Loop Line (Approx. 15 min. 220 JPY including Shin-Osaka -> Osaka)
  3. Tsuruhashi Station -> Nagase Station by Kintetsu Osaka Line (Approx. 8 min. 210 JPY)
  4. On foot (Approx. 10 min.)
Train
  1. Shin-Osaka Station -> Namba Station by Osaka Metro Midosuji Line (Approx. 15 min. 280 JPY)
  2. Namba Station -> Tsuruhashi Station by Kintetsu Nara Line (Approx. 6 min)
  3. Tsuruhashi Station -> Nagase Station by Kintetsu Osaka Line (Approx. 9 min. 260 JPY including Namba -> Tsuruhashi)
