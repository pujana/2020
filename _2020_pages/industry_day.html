Industry day

The industry day is an open day (free entrance) with topics provided by and focused on the games industry and how it links to research in games. The industry day takes place on the first day of IEEE CoG (24th August 2020). For information about the venue, visit Venue

The industry day will include:

  • Invited keynote talks from top figures in the field.
  • Talks from game companies and developers.
  • Live demo session.
